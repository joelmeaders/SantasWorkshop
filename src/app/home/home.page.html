A Toy for Every Girl and Boy

Welcome to the Denver Santa Claus Shop Pre-registration where we plan on fulfilling our mission even during the pandemic! This toy give-away program is available to Denver residents on Dec. 11, 12, 14, & 15.

Please follow the prompts to pre-register so that the distribution process can go quickly and save you time the day of the event. This year will be a mobile drive through toy distribution. You will receive toys based on your child’s age and gender.

If this is your first visit to the preregistration page, select "Create Account" and follow the instructions.

If you have already created an account and are visiting to change an existing registration, you will need to select "Sign In" and follow those instructions. Any change or addition will delete the first entry, so be sure to re-enter all information.

We look forward to providing your family with some Holiday cheer during these trying times.
